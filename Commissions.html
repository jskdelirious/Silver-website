Comissions


Art, to me, is a way of connecting with the world and expressing emotions and experiences. I hope my work resonates with you, and if you want to discuss a potential collaboration, please don't hesitate to reach out.


What I wont draw


  • - Full furries (I can draw cat ears/tails but not more than that)
  • - Animals (unless it's a simple one/accesory)
  • - Hateful art (Homophobia, Transphobia, Pedophilia etc)

Stuff that is hard for me to draw but I can draw


  • - Detailed backgrounds
  • - Machines
  • - Beards and Mustaches
  • - Nudity (Only to an extent)
  • - Anything not human
